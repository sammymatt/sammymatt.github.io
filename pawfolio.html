Pawfolio

The all-in-one pet management app to keep your furry friends healthy and happy.

Everything for your pets

Pawfolio brings all your pet care needs into one beautifully designed iOS app.

📋

Pet Profiles

Create detailed profiles for each of your pets with photos, breed info, and important details all in one place.

💊

Health Records

Track vaccinations, medications, vet visits, and health history. Never miss an important health milestone.

🔔

Reminders

Set smart reminders for feeding, medications, grooming, and vet appointments. Your pets will thank you.

📈

Weight Tracking

Monitor your pet's weight over time with visual charts to keep them in tip-top shape.

📸

Photo Journal

Capture and organise precious memories with a dedicated photo journal for each of your pets.

☁️

Cloud Sync

All your data securely backed up and synced across your devices. Your pet records are always with you.

Built with care

Pawfolio is built natively for iOS with a Python backend and reliable cloud infrastructure.
